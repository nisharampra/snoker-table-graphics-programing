8-Ball Pool Game

Game Rules:

  • Aim and shoot the cue ball using the mouse to pot the colored balls into the pockets.
  • Each colored ball has a different point value:
    • Red: 1 point
    • Yellow: 2 points
    • Green: 3 points
    • Brown: 4 points
    • Blue: 5 points
    • Pink: 6 points
    • Black: 7 points
  • Potting the cue ball or the black ball ends the game.
  • Use the 'Reset Game' button to start a new game.
  • Click 'Start Recording' to capture screenshots for recording.
